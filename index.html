About Us

Created in 2025 by a group of lifelong friends and passionate developers, Gimma was founded with one simple idea: make games we’d love to play ourselves. What started as a side project quickly turned into a full-time adventure in creativity, collaboration, and community. With a deep love for game mechanics, storytelling, and user experience, we set out to design, develop, and manage high-quality games for the Roblox platform — games that are not only fun to play, but also built to last.

Our mission is to create immersive worlds that players can get lost in, laugh in, and come back to again and again. Whether it's fast-paced action, challenging simulators, or quirky multiplayer experiences, we focus on delivering titles that stand out from the crowd. At Gimma, we believe that if we enjoy making the game — and you enjoy playing it — then we’ve done our job right.
